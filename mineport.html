About YLX Studio

Studio information and designer notes are arranged as a quiet editorial field.

YLX Studio works between architecture, visual narrative, and atmospheric composition. The practice focuses on restraint, spatial clarity, and the emotional weight carried by material, light, and proportion.

The studio develops visual worlds that feel calm, deliberate, and immersive. Every frame is treated like a spatial composition, where silence, interval, and rhythm are as important as the object itself.

YLX Studio is an independent design practice based in Taipei, developing work across spatial design, image direction, and concept-driven visual systems.

The studio is interested in architectural storytelling, editorial calm, and immersive visual pacing.

Yang Linxuan

Spatial designer / visual storyteller working with atmosphere, layout, and narrative-sensitive composition.
